Our Mission

Adding value to jackfruit and local bananas with lasting impact

At Garci, our mission goes beyond food. By adding value to jackfruit and pairing it with the goodness of local bananas, we are protecting Sri Lanka's age-old jackfruit trees while giving families a sustainable, nutritious alternative.

Our Promise

At Garci, we are committed to bringing the world authentic, healthy foods that celebrate the natural goodness and humble heritage of Sri Lanka. Our promise goes beyond taste - it is about purpose. By adding value to Sri Lanka's treasured jackfruit and pairing it with the wholesome richness of local bananas, we help protect age-old jackfruit trees while supporting local farmers and empowering our communities.

Every product we create is crafted with care, rooted in sustainability, and inspired by the tropical abundance of our island. With every bite, you're not only choosing a nutritious, innovative, vegan-friendly food - you're helping preserve a legacy and promote Sri Lankan goodness across the world.

Founder Note

A word from the founder

As the founder of Garci, my vision goes far beyond building a business. I want to carry the true value of Sri Lanka to the world.

Our island is blessed with some of the richest tropical soil on Earth, nurturing natural trees that have sustained our people for generations. Among them, the giant jackfruit trees stand as a symbol of our heritage, yet their true worth is often overlooked.

Through Garci, I aim to protect these trees and bring their value to global recognition. With over 14 years of experience in the software industry, I am committed to using my knowledge, discipline, and creativity to transform this idea into a meaningful, sustainable success.

Chathuraka Mallawa Arachchi
